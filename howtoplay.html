How to play

Rock paper scissors is a game played between two people. Each player chooses one of three moves: rock, paper, or scissors. The winner is determined by the rules: rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. If both players choose the same move, it's a tie.

First button is rock, second button is paper, and the third button is scissors

1. Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking one of the buttons.
2. The computer will randomly choose its move.
3. Rock beats Scissors.
4. Scissors beats Paper.
5. Paper beats Rock.
6. The winner will be displayed instantly.
